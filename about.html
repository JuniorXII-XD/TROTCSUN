About Us

More Than Just
a Club

TROTCS UN is a student-led innovation hub where creativity meets technology. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small group of passionate students into a thriving community of over 150 members dedicated to exploring the frontiers of technology, design, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

To empower students with the skills, mindset, and community to transform innovative ideas into impactful realities.

Our Vision

To be the leading student innovation collective, recognized for cultivating the next generation of creative leaders and problem solvers.

Our Journey

The TROTCS UN Timeline

From a small idea to a thriving community — trace the milestones that shaped who we are.

September 2020

Club Founded

TROTCS UN was born with 12 founding members and a vision to bridge creativity and technology in our school.

March 2021

First Hackathon

Organized our inaugural 24-hour hackathon with 50 participants and 3 industry mentors.

November 2022

National Recognition

Won first place at the National Student Innovation Challenge, putting TROTCS UN on the map.

June 2023

Community Launch

Launched our community coding program, teaching 200+ younger students the fundamentals of programming.

February 2024

International Competition

Represented our school at the International Youth Tech Summit in Singapore.

Present

150+ Members & Growing

Now a thriving ecosystem of innovators with 6 specialized divisions and partnerships with industry leaders.
